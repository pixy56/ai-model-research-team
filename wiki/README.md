# AI Model Research Wiki

This is the knowledge base for the AI Model Research Team.

## Structure

- **models/** - Individual model information
  - **llms/** - Large Language Models
  - **multimodal/** - Multimodal models
  - **reasoning/** - Reasoning-focused models
- **benchmarks/** - Benchmark information and scores
- **labs/** - Research lab profiles and releases
- **architectures/** - Architecture patterns and innovations
- **research-findings/** - Key research insights

## Usage

This wiki is maintained by the Literature Review Agent and Writing Agent.
Knowledge is ingested from:
- arXiv papers
- Lab announcements
- Benchmark leaderboards
- Analysis notebooks
