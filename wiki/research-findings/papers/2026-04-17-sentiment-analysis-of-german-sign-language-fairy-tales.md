# Sentiment Analysis of German Sign Language Fairy Tales

## Summary

We present a dataset and a model for sentiment analysis of German sign language (DGS) fairy tales. First, we perform sentiment analysis for three levels of valence (negative, neutral, positive) on German fairy tales text segments using four large language models (LLMs) and majority voting, reaching an inter-annotator agreement of 0.781 Krippendorff's alpha.
