# AtManRL: Towards Faithful Reasoning via Differentiable Attention Saliency

## Summary

Large language models (LLMs) increasingly rely on chain-of-thought (CoT) reasoning to solve complex tasks. Yet ensuring that the reasoning trace both contributes to and faithfully reflects the processes underlying the model's final answer, rather than merely accompanying it, remains challenging. We introduce AtManRL, a method that leverages differentiable attention manipulation to learn more faithful reasoning through reinforcement learning.
